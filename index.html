Yibiao YANG 杨已彪

Dr. Yibiao YANG is currently a Tenure-track Assistant Professor (准聘助理教授 & 特聘研究员 & 博导) in School of Computer Science at Nanjing University, and a faculty member of Software Quality Research Group, led by Prof. Baowen Xu.

He received his Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University in September 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Yuming Zhou.

His current research interest include AI+SE (Large-language Model+SE), automated testing and analysis technologies for software and systems.
