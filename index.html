Yibiao YANG(杨已彪)

Dr. Yibiao YANG is currently a Tenure-track Assistant Professor (准聘助理教授/特聘研究员/博导) in School of Computer Science at Nanjing University, and a faculty member in the Software Quality Research Group, led by Prof. Baowen Xu.

He received his Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University in September 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Yuming Zhou. He was a visiting student in CREST Research Group at University College London, co-supervised by Prof. Mark Harman and Prof. Jens Krinke.

His current research interest include AI+SE (Large-language Model+SE), automated testing and analysis technologies for software and systems.
