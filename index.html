Yibiao YANG (杨已彪)

Yibiao YANG is a Tenure-track Assistant Professor and Ph.D. advisor (准聘助理教授/特聘研究员/博导) in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University under the supervision of Prof. Yuming Zhou. He was a visiting student at the CREST centre, University College London, co-supervised by Prof. Mark Harman and Prof. Jens Krinke. His current research interests include AI for Software Engineering (e.g., Large Language Models for SE), automated testing and debugging technologies for complex systems.
