Yibiao YANG (杨已彪)

Yibiao YANG is currently a Tenure-track Assistant Professor and a Ph.D. advisor (准聘助理教授/特聘研究员/博导) in School of Computer Science at Nanjing University, and a faculty member in the Software Quality Research Group, led by Prof. Baowen Xu.

He received his Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University, under the supervision of Prof. Yuming Zhou. He was a visiting student in CREST centre at University College London, co-supervised by Prof. Mark Harman and Prof. Jens Krinke.

His current research interest include AI+SE (Large Language Model+SE), automated testing and analysis technologies for software and systems.
